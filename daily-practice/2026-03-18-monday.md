### 数学答案

**一、实数运算**
1. $|-3| + \sqrt{16} - 2^2 = 3 + 4 - 4 = 3$
2. $(-2)^3 + \sqrt[3]{-8} \times 2 = -8 + (-2) \times 2 = -8 - 4 = -12$
3. $\frac{1}{2} + (-\frac{2}{3}) - (-\frac{1}{6}) = \frac{3}{6} - \frac{4}{6} + \frac{1}{6} = 0$
4. $(-1)^{2024} + (-1)^{2023} = 1 + (-1) = 0$

**二、整式运算**
1. $3x^2 - 2x + 5 - 2x^2 - 3x + 1 = x^2 - 5x + 6$
2. $2a - 2b + 3a + 6b = 5a + 4b$
3. $(2x^2y)^3 \div (4x^3y^2) = 8x^6y^3 \div 4x^3y^2 = 2x^3y$
4. $x^2 - 9 = (x+3)(x-3)$

**三、解方程**
1. $3x - 7 = 2x + 5$ → $x = 12$
2. $2(x - 3) = 5x + 1$ → $2x - 6 = 5x + 1$ → $-3x = 7$ → $x = -\frac{7}{3}$
3. $\begin{cases} x + y = 5 \\ 2x - y = 4 \end{cases}$ → 相加得 $3x = 9$ → $x = 3$，代入得 $y = 2$

**四、应用题**
解：设最低可以打 x 折销售。
售价 = $120 \times \frac{x}{10}$
利润率 = $\frac{120 \times \frac{x}{10} - 80}{80} \geq 20\%$
$12x - 80 \geq 16$
$12x \geq 96$
$x \geq 8$
答：最低可以打 8 折销售。
